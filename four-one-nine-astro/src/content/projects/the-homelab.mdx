I first put together a server when I was about 14 so that I could transfer files from my computer to my phone wirelessly over my network. Since then, my home's arrangement of computers and equipment has grown not just in complexity, but also capability. When I began taking an interest in securing my privacy from the ever-encroaching data-munchers we call Big Tech, the more I began seeing the value in putting in the effort to host it myself.
